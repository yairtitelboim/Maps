CITY OF LOS ANGELES

ZONING ORDINANCE EXCERPT

R1 ZONE: Single Family Residential
Height limit: 35 feet
FAR: 0.45
Setbacks: Front 20 feet, Side 5 feet, Rear 15 feet
Allowed uses: Single family homes, parks, playgrounds
Prohibited uses: Multi-family housing, commercial buildings

C2 ZONE: Commercial
Height limit: 75 feet
FAR: 1.5
Setbacks: None required except when adjacent to residential
Allowed uses: Retail, restaurants, offices, hotels
Prohibited uses: Heavy industrial, manufacturing

Downtown Commercial District encompasses blocks between Main Street and Broadway.
Harbor Hills neighborhood is predominantly zoned R1.
